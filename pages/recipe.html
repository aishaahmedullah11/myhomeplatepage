Breakfast Recipes

Halwa Puri

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Semolina
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Boiled Chickpeas

Recipe

Prepare sweet halwa with semolina, sugar, and ghee. Fry fresh puris until golden brown. Serve hot with spicy chana for a traditional Pakistani breakfast.

Chana Masala

Ingredients

  • Boiled Chickpeas
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Pakistani Spices

Recipe

Cook onions, tomatoes, garlic, and spices. Add boiled chickpeas and simmer for 15–20 minutes. Serve with puri or naan.

Paratha & Omelette

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Recipe

Prepare a soft paratha on a hot pan. Cook the omelette with eggs, salt, and black pepper. Serve together while hot.

Sweet Lassi

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Sugar
  • Cold Water
  • Ice Cubes

Recipe

Blend yogurt, sugar, cold water, and ice until creamy. Serve chilled.

Lunch Recipes

Chicken Biryani

Cook chicken with onions, tomatoes, yogurt, and spices. Layer with boiled rice and steam until fully cooked.

Chicken Karahi

Cook chicken with tomatoes, ginger, garlic, green chilies, and traditional spices until thick and flavorful.

Daal Chawal

Boil lentils with turmeric and salt. Prepare garlic tempering and pour over the daal. Serve with steamed rice.

Dinner Recipes

Beef Nihari

Slow-cook beef with nihari spices for several hours until tender. Garnish with ginger, lemon, and coriander.

Mutton Karahi

Cook mutton with tomatoes, garlic, ginger, green chilies, and spices until the meat becomes soft.

Haleem

Cook meat, wheat, and lentils together for several hours. Blend well and garnish with fried onions and lemon.

Dessert Recipes

Kheer

Cook rice with milk, sugar, and cardamom until thick. Garnish with almonds and pistachios.

Gulab Jamun

Prepare soft dough balls, fry until golden, and soak them in warm sugar syrup.

Ras Malai

Serve soft cheese patties in chilled sweet milk flavored with cardamom and saffron.

Gajar Ka Halwa

Cook grated carrots with milk, sugar, and ghee until soft. Garnish with dry fruits.

Drinks Recipes

Fresh Lime

Mix fresh lemon juice, sugar, cold water, and ice cubes. Stir well and serve chilled.

Mint Lemonade

Blend mint leaves, lemon juice, sugar, water, and ice until refreshing.

Rooh Afza

Mix Rooh Afza syrup with chilled milk or cold water. Add ice cubes before serving.
